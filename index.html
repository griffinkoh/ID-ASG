What we offer?

Got an enthusiastic mindset for wildlife and animals? Griffin Wildlife is the most suitable for you! Offering a wide variety of animals ranging from mammels, fishes and even reptiles. Griffin Wildlife provides a fun and enriching experience for you and your lvoed ones to enjoy their day while sightseeing animals such as the majestic lion, arapina and many more fascinating creatures.

Fun Facts!!

  • Over 2800 animals
  • More than 300 species
  • 26% are threatened
  • 3-in-1 Park
  • 28000 visitors annually
  • SG Clean Certified
