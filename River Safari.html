What we offer?

Explore wonders of the largest tropical rainforest in the world and bring back unforgettable experiences

Wondering what life in Amazon's flooded rainforest is like? In the river safari, we
offer many wonders of the river and forest by showcasing rare and never seen before animals such as, the alligator gar, everyone's favourite panda, the longest alligator known to cross
the waters 'Yangtze Alligator', Caribbean flamingoes, Arapina and many more fascinating creatures that will amaze you!
